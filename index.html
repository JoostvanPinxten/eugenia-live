EuGENia Live

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

EuGENia Live is a flexible graphical modeling tool, it allows you to:

  • Quickly and easily define your modeling language
  • Change your modeling languages as easily as you change your models
  • Animate your modeling elements graphically
  • Store your models in your browser
  • Simulate your models with custom behavior

This project has been created by @louismrose and we have worked on it together to add animation and simulation properties during the YCCSA Summer School of 2013.

Check out the wiki for more information on using or developing this tool!
